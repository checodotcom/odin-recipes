Homemade Pizza Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 cups flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 1 packet (2 1/4 teaspoons) active dry yeast

Steps

  1. Activate Yeast
  2. Make Dough
  3. Knead Dough
  4. Let Dough Rise
  5. Shape and Bake
