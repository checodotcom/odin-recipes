Homemade Lasagna Recipe

Al dente lasagna noodles, flavorful ricotta cheese mixture, tomato sauce with Italian sausage, and gooey layers of cheese. Absolutely bursting with flavor, this hearty lasagna is heaven in a casserole dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 5 ounces pancetta, diced into small bits
  • 1 pound hot italian sausage
  • 1 cup chopped onion

Steps

  1. Gather ingredients
  2. Cook the Noodles
  3. Prepare the Filling
  4. Make the Cheese mixture
  5. Layer the Lasagna
  6. Bake
  7. Rest and serve
